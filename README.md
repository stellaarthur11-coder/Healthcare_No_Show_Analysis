# Healthcare No Show Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes healthcare appointment no show patterns for True North Health Network. The goal was to identify key trends behind missed appointments and present clear insights through an Excel dashboard.

The analysis focused on appointment volume, no show rates, wait time patterns, location performance, clinic performance, specialty performance, and patient demographics.

## Business Problem

Missed healthcare appointments can affect patient care, clinic scheduling, staff productivity, and revenue. This project explores where no shows are happening most often and what factors may be contributing to the problem.

## Key Metrics

Total Appointments: 3,000
Total No Shows: 430
Overall No Show Rate: 21.5%

No show rate by clinic branch

## Key Insights

The overall no show rate was 21.5%, showing that missed appointments are a meaningful operational issue.

Wait time appeared to be an important factor, with the average wait time at 18.6 days.

Some clinic branches and specialties had higher no show patterns than others, which can help leadership focus on targeted follow up, scheduling improvements, and patient communication.

## Dashboard Features

KPI summary cards
Overall no show rate visualization
Filters for country, city, clinic branch, specialty, and gender
Visual breakdown of no shows across major business categories

## Recommendations

Improve patient reminders before appointment dates.

Review clinics and specialties with higher no show rates.

Monitor wait time patterns and explore ways to reduce long scheduling gaps.

Use dashboard filters regularly to identify high risk areas and support better operational decisions.
